Grant Pemberton

A 2017 graduate of Tufts University, Grant Pemberton is eager to embark on a new learning experience in his first job. At Tufts, Grant majored in Biomedical Engineering and minored in Computer Science. He developed gpSciences in the Fall of 2016. This rapid prototyping company aims to address the needs of several new projects from a physical engineering and computer science standpoint.
